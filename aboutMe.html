Our Passion is Polished Concrete

Galaxy Concrete Polishing & Grinding use the latest Husqvarna machinery and premium systems with continued up to date research and development in the latest techniques & technology to achieve the finest results. With over 40 years of experience within the construction & building industry we have the understanding, skills and know how to handle any polished concrete or concrete grinding project big or small, treating each project with pride and professionalism, working in with our clients work schedules to manage that our projects run smoothly and on time.

At Galaxy Concrete Polishing & Grinding our aim is to always deliver a reliable service to our customers that is of the highest quality. We have strong relationships within our industry with a client base including Builders, Architects, Retail Shop Fitout/Fitters, Commercial & Retail businesses, and our valued private renovators and owner builders.

The Galaxy Team provide the highest work standards and can cater to all jobs from large commercial or retail projects to indoor and outdoor areas for residential homes. Attention to detail and our Customers' needs and expectations are always our priority and you can have confidence knowing that your project and final result is as important to us as it is to you.

The Galaxy Team will run through your job with you and assess the floors you wish to polish, discussing the different options available and processes involved, from the preparation of the flooring to the completion of your selected process to suit your style and budget.

Whether you’re Renovating your Home or Office, building your new Dream Home or if you’re looking for a dependable contractor to assist you with Polished Concrete flooring solutions for larger Residential or Commercial building projects, we are with you all the way.
