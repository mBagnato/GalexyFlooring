Grind & Seal

The GALAXY Grind and Seal process is an affordable flooring option that is suitable for both indoor and outdoor areas.
This is the cheaper option for a "Polished Concrete Look" and is a cost effective flooring commonly used in homes, retail outlets, cafes, restaurants, hardware stores & warehouses, factories and commercial properties just to name a few, as an attractive low maintenance, hard wearing flooring finish that is resistant to many elements.
Grind and Seal is a process of grinding concrete to the desired stone exposure level removing as many scratches as possible, filling minor cracks and surface imperfections to give the concrete a smooth level appearance, and then applying a clear or coloured high quality protective sealer to enhance wear and durability.

Levels of Stone / Aggregate exposure

  • A full exposure gives a full stone effect,
  • Random exposure is where there are variances of stone showing
  • "Salt and Pepper" or Light Random exposure is when the concrete
    is lightly ground so that minimal stone is seen
  • Coloured sealers are opaque (not clear) therefore aggregate exposure is unseen
  • Options for exposure level may vary depending on the condition of the concrete

Grind and Seal - Maintenance

  • Coatings take 5-7 days to fully cure
  • Keep internal floors vacuumed and dirt free
  • Protective pads under furniture is recommended
  • Micro fibre mops are best for a streak free cleaning result
  • If using a cleaning solution use a PH Neutral Floor Care cleaning solution and rinse with
    clean water
  • Spills should be cleaned as soon as possible
  • Clean outdoor areas by sweeping - if desired hose down & sweep excess water
  • Generally Sealers will need to be re-applied every 5-7 years depending on traffic

The Grind & Seal Process

  • Preliminary Grind with Coarse 30 Grit diamond abrasives for even surface level
  • Preliminary Grind to Expose Aggregate/Stone to desired level of exposure, which is the
    amount of stone that you can see in the finished result
  • Grind with Medium 60 Grit diamond abrasives to remove as many scratches as possible
  • Fill minor cracks and holes as required
  • Grind with fine 120 Grit diamond abrasives
  • Apply 2-3 coats of clear or coloured sealer
  • Allow 5-7 days to fully cure
