Polished Concrete Floors Melbourne

Polished concrete flooring from GALAXY is a beautiful long term flooring solution that give floors both inside and out a high end fresh contemporary look that is stylish and luxurious.

Concrete polishing is a process of grinding concrete until it is smooth to give floors a seamless terrazzo-stone appearance.

As well as being impressive in appearance, our polished concrete floors are a wonderful alternative to other floor coverings with many long term benefits. Polished Concrete floors are highly durable, scratch resistant and hygienic as well as being low maintenance and easy to clean.
